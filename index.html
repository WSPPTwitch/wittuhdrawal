ABOUT THE GAME

Withdrawals VR is an indie virtual reality game set in late 2000s/early 2010s, where you build your own drug empire from little to nothing. Start small with just a crappy motel room and some low quality pots, you'll work your way up through weed, shrooms, meth, coke, and finally MDMA. You will be able to purchase better properties (and maybe vehicles you can cook in, but i dunnnooooo if we'll get a grip on that lol)

THE STORY

You play as a redneck in his 20's trying to escape a broken home. Your abusive father grew weed and drank himself stupid, while your grandpa used you in his meth operation. Your messed up childhood is exactly where your knowledge comes from. You've got no car, no house, nothing.. Just a tiny motel room with some cheap pots and a dream.

As you build reputation with the right people, you'll unlock contacts who'll share their recipes - for a price. Make enough money and eventually you might just make it out of this life. Or get deeper in. Your call.
